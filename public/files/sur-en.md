# SÜR — On-Demand Driver App

**Platform:** iOS & Android (React Native / Expo)  
**Store:** Closed Alpha live on Google Play Store  
**Company:** Sür Teknoloji ve Sürücü Aracılık Hizmetleri Ltd. Şti.  
**Role:** Founder & Lead Developer  
**Stack:** React Native, Expo, real-time location, Google Play Store  
**Vision:** *"Güvenli Eğlence, Güvenli Dönüş"* — Safe Enjoyment, Safe Return

---

## Problem

Drunk driving is one of the leading causes of traffic fatalities. Yet the existing alternatives are deeply inadequate:

1. **No good alternative** — Users either can't find a taxi, don't want to leave their car behind, or can't trust it to be safe overnight. These friction points push people toward risky driving.
2. **Leaving your car behind** — Standard taxi/rideshare apps get you home, but your car stays at the venue. Retrieving it the next day wastes time and money.
3. **Tow truck chaos** — Existing tow truck services are fragmented, inconsistently priced, and hard to find quickly in an emergency.

---

## Solution

SÜR is a **location-based marketplace app** that connects users with verified private drivers and tow trucks — so users get home safely *in their own car*.

The model is simple: you're at a restaurant, beach club, or event and you don't want to drive. You open SÜR, a verified driver comes to you, drives your car to your destination with you inside, and goes home. Your car never leaves your sight.

**Pilot region:** İzmir — Urla, Çeşme, Alaçatı corridor. High nightlife and summer tourism density, ideal for launch.

---

## How It Works

1. User opens the app and requests a driver or tow truck
2. Real-time matching with the nearest available verified driver
3. Live location tracking throughout the trip
4. Rating and review system after each ride
5. Full insurance coverage from start to finish — vehicle and passengers

---

## Technical Architecture

- **Mobile:** React Native + Expo — single codebase, iOS and Android
- **Real-time:** Live location tracking and driver-user matching
- **Safety layer:** Driver vetting — criminal record check, psychotechnical documents, and driving test (license alone is not sufficient)
- **Insurance:** Full coverage for the vehicle and passengers for the entire duration of each trip
- **Store:** Closed Alpha (Beta) live on Google Play Store — technically functional

---

## Business Model

| Revenue Source | Description |
|----------------|-------------|
| Driver subscriptions | Drivers pay a weekly or monthly flat fee to access the platform and receive job matches |
| Tow truck subscriptions | Same model for tow truck operators |

**Why this works:** Drivers keep 100% of trip earnings — no commission cut. This maximizes driver loyalty and incentivizes volume. The platform earns predictable, recurring subscription revenue independent of individual trip counts.

---

## B2B Partnerships — "SÜR Noktası"

Restaurants, nightclubs, beach clubs, and event organizers partner with SÜR to offer the service as an on-site amenity. Venues advertise a *"SÜR Noktası"* (SÜR Point), allowing their customers to book a safe ride home directly from the venue. Partners enhance their brand value by actively promoting safe drinking culture.

---

## Competitive Advantage

- **Your car, your trip** — The user never has to leave their vehicle behind, unlike any taxi or rideshare alternative
- **Verified drivers** — Stricter than a standard driving license: criminal background check, psychotechnical evaluation, and practical driving test
- **Full insurance** — Every ride is covered from pickup to drop-off
- **Social impact** — Fewer drunk driving incidents means less burden on emergency services and healthcare systems
