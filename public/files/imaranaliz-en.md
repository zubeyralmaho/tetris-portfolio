---

## Problem

Obtaining zoning status (*imar durumu*) information for a land parcel in Turkey means navigating a slow, fragmented bureaucratic process: submitting petitions to multiple municipalities, waiting weeks for responses, and still receiving data that's difficult to interpret without expert knowledge.

Three core pain points:

1. **Difficult access** — Zoning data is locked inside municipality offices, requiring in-person visits, paperwork, and follow-ups that can take weeks.
2. **Information gap** — Even when data is obtained, investors and property owners lack the tools to interpret complex zoning parameters (KAKS, TAKS, floor count limits, etc.), leading to costly mistakes.
3. **Investment risk** — Outdated or misunderstood zoning data causes real financial damage: failed projects, legal disputes, and fraudulent land sales.

---

## Solution

İmarAnaliz is an **E-Ekspertiz (digital appraisal) platform** that digitizes Turkey's complex zoning regulations and delivers verified, official zoning analysis reports for land parcels in İzmir, Aydın, and Muğla — instantly, online, 24/7.

A user enters their parcel details, pays securely, and receives a structured report within seconds. No office visits. No waiting. No bureaucracy.

---

## How It Works

1. User searches their land parcel on the platform
2. Selects the desired report type
3. Pays via PayTR (credit card, instant processing)
4. Receives a detailed zoning report immediately — archived in their personal dashboard, downloadable as PDF at any time

---

## Technical Architecture

- **Frontend:** Next.js — fully responsive, SEO-optimized, mobile-first
- **Payment:** PayTR secure payment gateway — instant credit card processing
- **User Dashboard:** Personal report archive — all purchased reports stored, accessible and re-downloadable as PDF
- **Data Layer:** Curated datasets sourced directly from official municipality (Belediye) and land registry (TKGM) records for İzmir, Aydın, and Muğla
- **SEO:** Technical SEO implementation for organic land investor traffic

## Business Model

| Segment | Model | Description |
|---------|-------|-------------|
| B2C | Pay-per-report | Individual investors purchase single reports on demand |
| B2B | Bulk credit packages | Real estate agencies buy credit bundles at discounted unit cost |
| B2B | Monthly subscription | Construction firms access high-volume queries under a fixed monthly fee |

**Unit economics:** The product is a digital data report — zero inventory, zero logistics cost. High margin per transaction, scalable with minimal operational overhead.

---

## Competitive Advantage

- **Official data only** — Reports grounded in municipality and TKGM records, not speculation
- **Single-tool solution** — Eliminates weeks of bureaucratic back-and-forth; the process now takes seconds
- **Public benefit** — Ends information asymmetry in the real estate market; both buyers and sellers access the same verified data, reducing fraud and legal disputes

---

## Target Market

- **B2C:** Individual land investors, Turkish diaspora investors, property heirs verifying zoning status
- **B2B:** Real estate consultants, licensed appraisers, construction and development firms in the Aegean region — Turkey's most active real estate market
